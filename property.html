Cascade Apartment 4

Baw Baw Village, Mt Baw Baw, VIC 3833 (94 reviews) Apartment

Welcome to Cascade Apartment 4, a fully self-contained, solid-brick apartment set over two levels in the heart of Baw Baw Village at Mt Baw Baw. Nestled among snowgum trees with direct ski-in/ski-out access, this alpine retreat offers the perfect base for all seasons — from winter skiing to summer hiking and mountain biking.

The lower level features a queen bed, single bunk bed, TV, and en-suite bathroom. The upper level has a double bed plus single bed (tri-bunk) with a second bathroom across the hallway. The spacious open-plan living and dining area includes a gas log fireplace, a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, washing machine, and a drying room for wet ski gear. Entertainment options include a radio and DVD player.

Please note: there is no resort WiFi available. During winter, there is no vehicle access to the village — luggage drop is at Carpark 1. Whether you're planning a winter ski escape, a summer alpine getaway, or a peaceful retreat in nature, Cascade Apartment 4 delivers premium comfort and convenience in one of Victoria's most spectacular alpine settings.
