iCal Sync

Sync your availability with external booking platforms

How iCal Sync works: iCal synchronisation allows you to import and export your property availability calendars using the industry-standard iCalendar format. By connecting your Cascade Apartment 4 calendar with external platforms like Airbnb, Booking.com, and VRBO, you can automatically keep availability in sync across all channels and prevent double bookings.

Export Your Calendar

Share your Cascade Apartment 4 availability calendar with external booking platforms. Copy the iCal export URL below and paste it into the external platform's calendar import settings to keep availability synchronised.

https://app.cascadeskiapartments.com/ical/export/property-1.ics

Supported platforms: Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO, Google Calendar, and any iCal-compatible service
