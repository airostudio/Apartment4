Description

Self-contained 2-bedroom apartment at Mt. Baw Baw Alpine Resort, sleeping up to 7 guests. Bedroom 1 features a queen bed with a bunk (double below, single above). Bedroom 2 has a tri-bunk (single, single, double). Gas log fireplace in the lounge, fully equipped kitchen, private ensuite bathroom and second bathroom with shower, separate laundry with washing machine and drying room. Private entrance with a snow play area and 3 toboggans included. Pillows and doonas provided — guests supply their own linen and towels. No resort WiFi; 4G available. Winter: no private vehicle access to resort village (shuttle from car park).
