About the Apartment

A cosy alpine retreat tucked around the back of the Cascade complex for extra privacy

Cascade Apartment 4 is a comfortable two-storey ski apartment located in Baw Baw Village at Mt Baw Baw, Victoria. The lower level features a queen bed, single bunk bed, TV, and en-suite bathroom. The upper level offers a double bed plus single bed (tri-bunk) with a second bathroom across the hallway. Enjoy the warmth of a gas log fireplace, a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, washing machine, and a drying room for your ski gear.

Please note: During winter, there is no vehicle access to the village. Luggage drop is at Carpark 1 with oversnow transport available at $40 per trip. There is no resort WiFi access.
